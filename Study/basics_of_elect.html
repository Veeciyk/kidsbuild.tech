What is Electricity? ‚ö°

Electricity is energy that flows like an invisible river inside wires. It's made up of tiny, invisible balls called electrons that move in a path called a circuit. When these electrons flow through things like lights or motors, they make them work!

üß† Fun Fact!

The human brain also uses tiny amounts of electricity to send messages! That's how you can think, move, and even blink your eyes.
