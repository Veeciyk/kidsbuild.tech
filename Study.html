Discover what robots are, how they differ from machines, and their real-life applications. Learn about essential robot parts like sensors, motors, and controllers, while understanding basic safety rules for handling tools.

Understand the concept of electricity, where it comes from, and how it flows. Learn about conductors, insulators, and fundamental electrical safety tips.

Explore the basics of voltage, current, and resistance using simple analogies. Get hands-on experience with batteries, breadboards, and key electronic components like resistors, LEDs, and switches.

Learn how robots move using different types of motors and how sensors help them interact with their environment. Experiment with motor control and light sensors.

Discover how microcontrollers like Arduino act as the "brain" of robots. Learn how to connect an Arduino to a computer and upload simple programs.

Explore block-based programming and Arduino C++. Understand how code controls robots and try simple coding activities with LEDs and buzzers.

Step-by-step guide to creating line-following robots using Arduino, sensors, and motors. Learn how to write, test, and debug robot code.

Enhance your robot with new features, improve performance, and complete a final project by designing your own custom robot.
